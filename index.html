Latent 4D Spatio-Temporal Representation Learning for Coherent 4D Sequence Generation

Anonymous submission

We propose an image-text conditioned 4D generation method with 3D shape quality, color fidelity and sequence coherence.

Abstract

Directly learning to model 4D content, including shape, color and motion, is challenging. Existing methods depend on skeleton-based motion control and offer limited continuity in detail.

To address this, we propose a framework that generates coherent 4D sequences with animation of 3D shapes under given conditions (text-image guidance) with dynamic evolution of shape and color over the spatial and temporal dimensions. We first employ a coherent 3D shape and color modeling strategy to encode shape and color information of each detailed 3D geometry frame into a latent space. The proposed latent 4D sequence representation allows efficient diffusion model operation and spatio-temporal attention. Finally, temporally coherent and high-fidelity 4D sequences are generated conforming well to the input images and text prompts. Extensive experiments on the ShapeNet, 3DBiCar and DeformingThings4D datasets for several tasks demonstrate that our method effectively learns to generate high quality 3D shapes with consistent color and coherent mesh animations, improving over the current state-of-the-art.
